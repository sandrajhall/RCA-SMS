Geoapify Address Autocomplete

The application uses a free service for address autocomplete.  The geoapify key was set up with my (Sandy Hall) email address.  If for any reason you need to generate a new key, that is fine.  Just replace my key in appsettings.json and appsettings.Development.json with a new key.
https://apidocs.geoapify.com/docs/geocoding/address-autocomplete/
